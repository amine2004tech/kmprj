About ESI

The École Supérieure d’Informatique (ESI) is a top-tier institution specializing in computer science, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and data science.

Why Choose ESI?

  • Innovative Curriculum: Cutting-edge courses in AI, cybersecurity, and software development.
  • Industry Partnerships: Collaborations with Google, Microsoft, and IBM.
  • State-of-the-Art Labs: Advanced facilities for hands-on learning.
  • Global Recognition: ESI graduates work in top tech companies worldwide.

Majors at ESI Rabat

ISSIC - Information & Communication

Specialized in digital media, journalism, and corporate communication strategies.

ISITD - Documentation & Archives

Expertise in digital libraries, document management, and archiving systems.

INN - Digital Transformation & Cybersecurity

Focus on cloud computing, IT security, and modern software development.

ICSD - Cybersecurity & Data Science

Advanced topics in AI, blockchain, and ethical hacking.
